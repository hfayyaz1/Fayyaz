Our Story

Half a Century of
Textile Mastery

Founded in 1975 in Karachi, Fayyaz Enterprises has grown from a regional procurement agent into a trusted international sourcing partner. For over five decades, we have bridged the gap between global buyers and Pakistan's world-class manufacturing capabilities.

Our deep-rooted relationships with vetted factories across Pakistan's major textile hubs — combined with our meticulous quality oversight — ensure that every shipment reflects the excellence our clients expect.

1975
Founded in Karachi as a local buying agency, establishing foundational factory partnerships across Pakistan.
1990s
Expanded into European markets, building long-term relationships with buyers in the Netherlands and Germany.
2000s
Entered North American and Pacific markets — USA, Canada, and New Zealand — scaling order volumes significantly.
Today
A full-service sourcing agency managing end-to-end apparel and home textile procurement across 4 continents.
